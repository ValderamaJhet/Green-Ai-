About This App

This app allows you to check the quality of your leafy vegetables and the data is supported in local crops from Benguet. GreenAi will ensure that our market has higher-quality fresh produce and will help you to identify the particular diseases.


Featured Vegetables

This will only work with the following

  • Bokchoy
  • Broccoli
  • Cabbage
  • Lettuce

Diseases that this app can detect

  • Soft Rot
  • Powdery Mildew
  • Leaf Drop
  • Downy Mildew
  • Bottom Rot
  • Big Vein
  • Alternaria leaf spot
  • White Rust
  • Black Rot
  • Bacterial Soft Rot
  • Pest Disease

Vegetable Classes

  • Class A. Fresh vegetables, marketable anywhere.
  • Class B. Slightly fresh vegetables. May have minor issues.
  • Class C. Rejected, not suitable for market.

How to take a picture?

  • Focus the Camera on the specific plant and make sure to avoid a blurry shot.
  • Make sure to focus on the leaves if you think the frame is big.
  • One specific plant only at a time.
